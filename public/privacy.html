Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

88 Venture Studio ("we," "us," or "our") operates an internal business management platform that connects to third-party services including QuickBooks Online. This privacy policy describes how we handle data accessed through these integrations.

Information We Access

When you authorize our application to connect to your QuickBooks Online account, we may access financial data including company information, account balances, transactions, invoices, customers, and vendors. This data is used solely for internal business management, reporting, and operational decision-making within 88 Venture Studio and its affiliated brands.

How We Use Your Information

All data accessed through QuickBooks Online is used exclusively for:

  • Internal financial reporting and analysis
  • Business operations management across our portfolio of brands
  • Automated bookkeeping summaries and alerts for company leadership

Data Storage and Security

Financial data is processed on our private infrastructure and is not shared with third parties. Access tokens are stored securely and encrypted at rest. We do not sell, rent, or distribute any financial data to external parties.

Data Retention

We retain financial data only as long as necessary for business operations. You may revoke access at any time by disconnecting the application from your QuickBooks Online account.

Third-Party Services

This application integrates with Intuit QuickBooks Online via their official API. Your use of QuickBooks Online is governed by Intuit's own privacy policy and terms of service.
